HEADER FIELDS
-------------
visitorName     Full name of the person booking.
visitorType     One of: "Regular" | "Guest Chef".
requestedStart  ISO-8601 start timestamp with timezone.
requestedEnd   ISO-8601 end timestamp with timezone.
email           Contact email for confirmation.
notes           Free-text; some rows use it to flag edge cases
                (EDGE:, MALFORMED:, SIMULATE_API_FAILURE).

ASSUMPTIONS
-----------
- Business hours:            08:00 – 18:00, Monday–Friday.
- Guest Chef prep buffer:    -60 minutes on start.
- Guest Chef cleanup buffer: +30 minutes on end.
- Conflict detection runs against BUFFERED (effective) times.
